There are 3 types of Signals:

  • Some Signals

  • Discrete Signals:- It is disturbed at few instances(i.e. it comes to zero at some point of time)
  • Continuous Signal:- It is not disturbed at any instances
  • Modulated Signal:-It is sum of both Discrete and Continuous Signals
